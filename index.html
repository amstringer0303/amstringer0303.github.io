Research

My doctoral work focuses on landfast sea ice dynamics and coastal climate risk in Arctic communities. I develop machine learning tools to detect sea ice edges using Sentinel-2 imagery and analyze freeze-up mechanisms along Arctic coastlines.

Kivalina — Master's Research

Master's research examining sea ice thickness and subsistence access using ICESat-2 altimetry and community interviews in Kivalina, Alaska.

Qikiqtarjuaq Field School

Field training in Arctic glaciology and environmental observation in Qikiqtarjuaq, Nunavut.

Utqiaġvik Research Scoping — Aug 2025

Field reconnaissance and research planning along Alaska’s Arctic coast.

Winter Fieldwork — Jan 2026

Sea ice observations and environmental data collection during winter conditions.

AGU Fall Meeting — Dec 2025
